Privacy Policy for ARIS TEAM CARS

ARIS TEAM CARS provides an offline Android application for viewing a local vehicle catalog, preparing rental estimates, and saving booking requests directly on the user's device.

Platform
Offline Android App
Core Principle

1 Data Collection

This application does not require account registration and does not collect personal data on our servers.

The app does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking technologies, or background data collection.

The app does not request dangerous Android permissions such as location, camera, microphone, contacts, or storage access.

2 Data Stored on Your Device

The application stores data locally on the device only. This may include:

  • booking request information that you enter, such as selected vehicle, rental dates, estimates, status, and optional notes;
  • local pricing preferences, such as the extra kilometer cost;
  • a local onboarding preference used to remember whether the welcome screen has already been shown.

3 Data Transmission

By default, the application does not transmit your local data to ARIS TEAM CARS or to third-party servers.

If you choose to export local data from the Settings screen, the exported file is created only after you select a destination using the Android system document picker. In that case, the file is written to the location or provider you choose.

If you choose to use Android sharing features, only the content you intentionally share is passed to the app or service you select.

4 Payments and Reservations

This application does not process payments and does not automatically confirm car rentals.

Booking requests saved in the app are local records for your own use and require direct confirmation with ARIS TEAM CARS.

5 Data Retention and Deletion

Your local data remains on your device until you delete it from the application settings or uninstall the application, subject to normal Android device behavior.

6 Children's Privacy

This application is not specifically directed to children and does not knowingly collect children's personal data.
